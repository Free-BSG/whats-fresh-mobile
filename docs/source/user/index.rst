How To Use Oregon's Catch
=========================

Oregon's Catch works a little bit like a Google\ :sup:`tm` \ search:
tell the app what to look for and it will reply with a list of search
results.

**Step 1.** To tell the app what to look for, use the home page.

**Step 2.** Once the app has found a list of search results, these results
may be viewed and explored on the list page. Tapping a result item
will bring up more detailed information about that specific item

**Step 3.** Detailed information about a specific item in the search
results is displayed on a detail page.

**Step 4.** Some products in the app\'s database contain educational
information this information is available on the education pages.
